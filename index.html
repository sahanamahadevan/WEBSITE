MY DESIGN PURPOSE



I exist in the design world to bring joy into people's lives. I do this by harnessing each step of the design process with a child-like wonder. Even in the most critical and challenging moments of a project ,to me, there is always a discovery within the ambiguity and the complexity. In a team, I'm a champion for positive attitude + thrive on the energy that brings happiness to others. As an innate reader of people's character and behavior, I'm often the glue that keeps the team together,negotiation and co-creation bring me pleasure. Leadership to me, is about bringing out the best in others. I'm always able to motivate myself through the gift of possibility: The possibilities are TRULY endless!
