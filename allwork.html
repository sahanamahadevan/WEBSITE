SPATIAL MAP FOR COPD CAREGIVERS

Fall 2017

My role: I worked on a project that was about identifying various roles a COPD caregiver has to play when taking care of a patient. I created this aerial view floorplan of the home of a patient, the orange bubbles signify the barries/problems or even places where a caregiver has to step in to help them. For instance, someone with the disease is always attached to the Oxygen tube and cooking near the gas is dangerous for them. Oxygen is combustible, so this person was resorting to microwaved food until when the caregiver arrives (based on real patient qualitative data, that was studied and mapped).

What was fun: I love mapping data! Making sense out of a complicated and large amount of quotes and insights, identifying patterns and pieces information in this manner gives me immense satisfaction.
